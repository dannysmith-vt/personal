About

Hello, I'm Danny, a sophmore computer science major and theatre minor at Middlebury College, Class of 2027. Originally from Vermont, I graduated from Woodstock Union High School in 2023, where I led the Social Action Club, served as Student Council Vice President, and performed at the Yoh Theater, among other activities. My interests are diverse, encompassing coding, running, and singing.

Activities

I am involved in a lot of things, some more than others. From playing Quadball to Mentoring my old robotics team, here are what is most captivating to me right now:

Archytas Robotics

I was a member of Archytas FRC Team 6933 for 6 years, four of which I spent as the lead scout. In my time, I spearheaded the development of a cutting-edge scouting data system, cementing my role as the team's strategy expert. My contributions extend beyond tactics; I now mentor students in coding and building the robot, as well as sharing my in-depth understanding of the game. Discover more about Archytas here.

Middlebury SIM

Middlebury SIM, or Stuck In the Middle, is my Acapella group. With around 10 members, we arrange all our own music, and sing anywhere from joint concerts with fellow groups to assisted living facilities in town. SIM has improved my singing and leadership abilities, and I am very glad I found such a fun group. Our current repertoire includes Loch Lomond, Haven't Met You Yet, and more. You can find our youtube here and website here.

Quadball

I have always loved Harry Potter, and when I found out the real life version of Quidditch existed, and was founded at Middlebury College, I knew I had to join. On the Middlebury Quadball team, I primarily play beater, and occasionally seeker. We compete in the Massechusetts Quadball Conference (MQC), and regulaly travel to the Boston area for tounraments. Quadball is a great sport and community, check out this link for an overview of its history.

Middlebrow Improv

The Middlebrow Society for Improvisational Comedy, also known as Middlebrow or Bräü, is one of two improv comedy groups on campus, focussing on short-from comedy. I joined my first semester, and have already participated in 4 shows. As well as putting on shows for the college community, Middlebrow also holds workshops, and we are planning on working with Sib2Sib this spring to hold events with local middleschoolers.

Other Bits and Bobs

My time spent does stop there. In addition to the 4 activities above and my classes, I am also a member of the Middlebury College Democrats, the Philosophy Club, Civics in Action, the Newman Catholic Club, and Architecture Table. I am working with other students on the founding of a fencing club, and I am member of the Collegiate Equestrian Team, although I am taking this semester off. I am also involved with Bethany Birches Camp in Plymouth, where I have been a counselor for the past 3 summers, and I volunteer at winter camps throughout the cold months.
